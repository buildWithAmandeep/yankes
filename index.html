About Yanke's Landscaping

Landscaping and Snow Removal Since 2004


Founded in 2004, Yanke's Landscaping has been delivering top-tier residential and commercial landscaping and snow removal services across Ontario. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and focus on customer satisfaction. With a skilled and dedicated team, we transform outdoor spaces into well-crafted, functional areas tailored to the unique needs of each property.

Our services encompass everything from landscape design, installation, and maintenance to comprehensive winter management solutions, ensuring that your property looks its best year-round. Our experienced crew is equipped with the latest tools and techniques to handle projects of all sizes, from small residential gardens to expansive commercial grounds.

At Yanke's Landscaping, we understand the demands of Ontario's changing seasons and work diligently to keep your outdoor spaces safe, clean, and accessible, even in the harshest winter conditions. Our snow removal team is available 24/7 during the winter months, providing timely and thorough service to keep your driveways, parking lots, and walkways clear of snow and ice.

By choosing Yanke's Landscaping, you are partnering with a company that values quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction above all. Let us bring our expertise to your property, ensuring it remains beautiful, safe, and functional through every season.
